About Us

Welcome to FOODHUB EATERY, your go-to destination for authentic Kenyan local dishes. We pride ourselves on serving delicious meals made from traditional recipes passed down through generations. Our mission is to provide a warm and inviting atmosphere where you can enjoy the rich flavors of Kenya.

Our Story

FOODHUB EATERY was founded with the vision of bringing the taste of Kenya to food lovers everywhere. Our chefs are passionate about using fresh, locally sourced ingredients to create dishes that celebrate the diverse culinary heritage of our country.

Our Menu

From hearty stews to flavorful grilled meats, our menu offers a wide variety of options to satisfy your cravings. Whether you're in the mood for a classic dish like Nyama Choma or something more adventurous, we have something for everyone.

Visit Us

We invite you to visit FOODHUB EATERY and experience the best of Kenyan cuisine. Whether you're dining with family, friends, or colleagues, we promise a memorable meal that will keep you coming back for more. We are located at Garden Street Plaza opposite Magunas supermarket, Chuka Town, Tharaka Nithi County
